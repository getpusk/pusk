# Pusk — Branding Guide

## Mascot: Soroka (Magpie / Сорока)

NORA = Chipmunk (stores supplies = artifacts)
Pusk = Magpie (talks = bot platform, delivers messages)

Why magpie:
- Talkative — messenger/bots
- Black and white — stylish minimalism
- "Soroka na khvoste prinesla" (Russian idiom: magpie brought it on her tail = news delivery)
- Recognizable in Russian culture instantly

## Logo Descriptions (for AI image generation)

### Main Logo
Minimalist magpie in profile, black and white with teal accent (#0f3460) on tail.
Geometric style, modern tech logo aesthetic. Holding a small chat bubble or envelope
in beak. Clean background, no text.

### Social Media / Blog Posts
Cartoon magpie mascot: friendly, big eyes, sitting on a wire/antenna.
One claw holding a smartphone with inline keyboard buttons.
Style: flat illustration, dark background (#1a1a2e), bright accents.
Text "Pusk" nearby.

### Favicon / App Icon (512x512)
Magpie head in circle, minimalist, 2 colors: dark blue background (#1a1a2e),
white magpie silhouette.
